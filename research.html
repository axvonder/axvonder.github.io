As a population health and sustainable food systems researcher, I focus on:

- epidemiologic analysis of diet–disease associations

- machine learning for improved disease risk prediction

- microsimulation modelling of health & environmental impacts of diet transitions

- tracking rapid shifts in dietary patterns

- translating research into policy with government partners
